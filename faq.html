Frequently Asked Questions

Are your gemstones certified?

Yes — all diamonds and precious stones come with a basic certificate. High-value stones include lab certification details.

What is your return / exchange policy?

Custom pieces are non-returnable. Standard items can be returned within 7 days in original condition (terms apply).

Do you offer resizing?

Yes — complimentary resizing within 15 days for selected items. For others, nominal charges apply.

Do you deliver nationwide?

We provide local delivery free and courier delivery across India with insurance for high-value items.
